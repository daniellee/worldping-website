raintank is an open-source monitoring platform that collects, stores and analyzes data about your infrastructure through a gorgeously powerful front-end.

infrastructure monitoring is broken.
(and it's geting worse)

It's gotten complicated, frustrating and expensive.

It's become a massive hallucination. People get bombarded with confusing alerts, so they can stare at nonsensical charts while half awake, just to see if a problem even exists.

data deluge Traffic's way up, but so is the amount of metrics, messages and alerts from your ever-growing stack.

terrible choices A proprietary solution? SaaS? It'll be expensive, and you'll lose control. Open source? Could be a time sink.

fractured vision You want correlation and collaboration, across your whole stack. Disparate tools and data make seeing the whole picture hard.

It doesn't have to be so bad.

raintank is fixing it.
