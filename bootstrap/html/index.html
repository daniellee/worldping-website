ridiculously easy network performance monitoring

Litmus is our first integration, and is a great way to get started with Grafana. Monitor the data quality of any web app from anywhere. Get a handle on your application performance and availability from all over the world. Keep your users happy by finding and fixing problems fast.

the power of grafana

Built on Grafana, Litmus is much more than just vanilla NPM. Use the power of Grafana to bring in your other data sources, and leverage its leading visualization capabilities to create the perfect view that brings it all together.
