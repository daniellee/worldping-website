it’s not about political beliefs. it’s not about cutting costs. it’s not because it’s the cool thing to do.

Simply put, we believe that open source is the best way to develop the most awesome software for our customers. We also think that all infrastructure software is destined to be open.

Our mission is to provide a fully supported, turnkey offering for our customers through our OpenSaaS platform. We want to help them avoid lock in, and take back control: hopefully a refreshing change in a world filled with proprietary SaaS options.

Everything we do is open-source. It’s better for our customers. Better for our employees. Better for the community.
