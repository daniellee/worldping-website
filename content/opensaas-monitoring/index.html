openSaaS monitoring
from the team behind Grafana.

We’re proud to announce raintank’s first open source plugin, Litmus network performance monitoring. Litmus can be used as a purely online monitoring service, or you can plug it directly into your existing Grafana as a data source.

Litmus continually tests, stores and alerts on the global performance and uptime of your Internet applications so you can spot problems and fix them immediately.


global monitoring

Monitor your site's or app's performance from all over the planet, across different networks and carriers.
