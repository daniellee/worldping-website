litmus continually monitors your global performance and uptime.

So you can keep your users happy.

reduce the noise

Litmus provides a clean backbone signal from around the world, and automatically learns what “normal” is so that you don’t have to.

RAINDEX, our dynamically computed index of performance from every Collector make your alerts even even cleaner and more actionable.

a global footprint you make your own

Monitor performance from all over the planet, across different networks and carriers with dozens of public Litmus collectors.

Install our open-source Collector software to measure the performance of/from your own datacenters and offices.

ridiculously easy

There’s no software or agent to install or configure. Be up and running with Litmus and Grafana in less than 60 seconds.

Just give us the domain name of your site or application, and we’ll automatically detect what to monitor.

pinpoint the problem quickly

Is the problem a regional network issue? DNS, yet again? A slowdown in the backend?

Litmus allows you to pinpoint the problem through gorgeous visualizations, giving you context across different layers of the stack.

get alerted

Sit back and relax; we’ll let you know when you experience a problem. We validate errors across multiple locations, so we won’t annoy you false positives.

the power of grafana

Built on Grafana, Litmus is much more than just vanilla NPM. Use the power of Grafana to bring in your other data sources, and leverage its leading visualization capabilities to create the perfect view that brings it all together.

real-time insight

Litmus gives you real time insight into exactly what’s going on. With 10 second monitoring, you can watch problems as they develop and get instant feedback as soon as they’re resolved.
