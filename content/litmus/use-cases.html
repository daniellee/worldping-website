anyone can use litmus to gain insight into their network performance

Litmus can be used by anyone wanting insight to the performance of their web apps. We thought some use cases might help you learn how to take advantage of this historical data.

devops

Your users are demanding, and they expect perfect uptime and performance. It’s your job to deliver. Your infrastructure is growing more complex every day, and it’s easy to get overwhelmed with confusing alerts.

Reduce MTTR and troubleshoot more effectively

Litmus can perform global testing as often as every 10 seconds, providing you with continuous positive validation of your uptime and performance. Unlike other solutions, you can be notified within seconds of a problem with valuable context about what kind of problem you’re dealing with.

Get quick and meaningful alerts

Litmus offers clean data and advanced alerting policies to make sure that you get timely, useful and actionable notifications every time.

Share data and collaborate

Litmus allows you to easily share interactive snapshots of exactly what you see with members of your team or third parties. Even if they don’t have a login they can see what you see.

service providers

XaaS depends on managing the quality of service between you and your users around the world. Your customers count on your infrastructure being up. You have SLAs to uphold, and it’s up to you to prove to your customers that your infrastructure is up to the task.

Get a complete perspective

Litmus provides visibility by testing from the outside-in and the inside-out. Deploy our open-source Collector within your own environment and measure all your dependencies. You can even measure performance from the perspective of your clients.

Find problems before they become issues

With continuous monitoring, Litmus can alert you with actionable alerts within seconds of a problem starting. Find and fix problems before your users notice, and improve your retention and customer satisfaction.

Collaborate with your vendors and partners

Stop playing the blame game with your ISPs and internal teams. Litmus allows you to let the data speak for itself; easily share an interactive version of what you see with snapshots.

site owners

Your website is your brand. Whether you have a complex globally distributed application, or a simple blog, you need to monitor it for performance and availability. Wouldn’t you like to keep an eye on your competition and see how your site compares? What about keeping your service providers honest, and making sure they’re living up to their SLAs?

Hold your providers accountable

Is your cloud provider meeting their SLA for uptime and performance? Which new cloud provider do you choose? Which CDN is going to best? How is the performance of your new site in Asia? Litmus can help you answer all these questions, even before your first user visit.

Find out how you stack up against the competition

Are you slowing down? Is your competition speeding up? Litmus allows you to get a handle on relative performance of your site and your competitors from the locations that matter to you.

Protect your brand

Downtime and sluggish performance is a direct hit to your brand. Litmus can send you extremely timely, high quality alerts so that you can take action immediately.
